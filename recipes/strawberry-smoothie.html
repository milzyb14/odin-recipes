Strawberry Smoothie

Recipe

A delicious smoothie to go along with the southwest tofu hash! This is a great way to get in some extra calories and make this breakfast tastier and more filling!

Ingredients
  • 2 cups frozen strawberries
  • 1 room temp banana
  • 1/4 cup greek yogurt
  • 1 cup milk or dairy alternative
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons maple syrup, honey, or agave syrup
  • 1/2 cup ice
Optional add-ins:
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla
  • fresh mint leaves
  • kale
  • spinach
